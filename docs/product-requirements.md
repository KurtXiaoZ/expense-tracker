## Overview

Expense Tracker is an app that helps users manage their finances by setting savings goals and tracking spending over time. Users can monitor whether their spending habits align with their goals, giving them a clear picture of their financial progress.

## Features

### 1. Income Setup

Users can define one or more income sources for a given savings period, specifying the expected amount for each source.

### 2. Savings Goal Setting

Users can set a savings target amount and the duration of the savings period (in months).

### 3. Budget Calculation

Based on the savings goal and anticipated income, the app automatically calculates the total available budget for the period and breaks it down into a monthly budget.

### 4. Funding Source Balances

Users can view their current balance across all funding sources to get a snapshot of where their money is.

### 5. Spending Records

Users can log, view, and update spending records. Each record includes:

- **Description** — what the expense was for
- **Amount** — how much was spent
- **Date** — when the expense occurred
- **Category** — a single category per record

Records are organized by month. Each month also includes a summary showing the monthly budget, total amount spent, and remaining balance.

### 6. Category Management

Users can create and manage categories to classify their spending records.
